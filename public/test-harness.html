This page spawns one host view and three auto-joined participants in iframes so you can see the full FBSN flow without a phone.

Click Create test room to start. You'll get:

  • Top pane: facilitator view (you, hosting)
  • Bottom 3 panes: auto-joined participants (Alice, Bob, Carol)
